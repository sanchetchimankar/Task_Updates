A computer is a device that accepts information

(in the form of digitalized data) and manipulates it for some result based on a program,

software, or sequence of instructions on how the data is to be processed.

Complex computers include the means for storing data (including the program,

which is also a form of data) for some necessary duration. A program may be invariable

and built into the computer hardware (and called logic circuitry as it is on microprocessors)

or different programs may be provided to the computer (loaded into its storage and

then started by an administrator or user). Today's computers have both kinds of programming.
